About this website

Academic context and disclaimer

Purpose of the website

This website was created to present student projects developed in an academic context within the ESAD Product Design course. It works as a simple static archive for workshop activities, experiments, and design outcomes.

Academic context

The pages published here are part of teaching and learning activities. Their purpose is educational, exploratory, and documentary. Projects may represent work in progress, prototypes, conceptual proposals, or early-stage design investigations.

Student responsibility

Each student is responsible for the contents of their own project page, including text, images, references, links, and any media uploaded to their folder.

Disclaimer

Any brand names, product references, visual materials, or third-party images that may appear in student pages are used only for academic discussion, analysis, or contextual reference. Their presence does not imply endorsement, partnership, or commercial use.

If any content needs correction, attribution, or removal, it should be reviewed by the course coordination or the student responsible for that page.
